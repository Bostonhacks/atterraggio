Demystifying the Cloud — Monday March 12th at 7:30pm

Video of Workshop and Slides

"The Cloud" has been everyone's favorite buzzword for a while, come learn about how it really works.

Andrew Hoang, ENG '18 and future production engineer at Facebook, will lead us through the many facets of cloud infrastructure. You'll learn how to create, configure, and launch a server. You'll also learn about how many different tools of the cloud, like nameservers, DNS, proxies, load balancing, and more, work together to make a server secure and performant.

If you're looking to launch a personal website, webapp, or other web service, this is the workshop for you. All skill levels welcome.

The workshop will be held at the new BUild Lab, located at 730 Commonwealth Ave. Enter through the doors under the red awning next to Pavement Coffee.
