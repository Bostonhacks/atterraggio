Come hack with us!

BostonHacks is Boston University's annual hackathon, and this coming Fall 2018 will be our fourth annual event! In Fall of 2017, we hosted 480 students from all around North America who formed teams and created some awesome projects in only 24 hours. Along the way, they learned about how to use various new technologies and built up their experience as developers.
